Game Rules & Gameplay

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • You are 'X', and your opponent is 'O'. Players take turns placing their marks.
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  • Winning awards **+50 points**. A draw awards **+10 points**. Matches reset immediately on finish.
